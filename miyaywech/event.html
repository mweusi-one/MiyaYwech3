21-Jan-26

8:00 - 11:00

MRF Mamboleo - Kisumu

Waste pickers training.

Waste Pickers play a big role on matters of Waste Management hence their efforts need to be recognized and appreciated! Today we begin the journey of empowering waste pickers from the informal settlements by training them on different types of wastes and how to handle them using Personal Protective Equipments (PPEs). This is one of the objectives of the "Upscalling Waste Management Systems in Kisumu City " being supported by the UMI Funds . This will be a regular activity and the identified and registered Waste Pickers will be supported with branded PPEs and enrolled in the Miya Ywech Sacco.
